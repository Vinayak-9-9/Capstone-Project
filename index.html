About The Game

A Battle for Survival

Cancer Hunter is more than just a game; it's a visualization of the battle happening within. Players take on the role of a microscopic guardian equipped with advanced medical weaponry.

Navigate through vital organs, identify malignant cells, and neutralize them before they spread. Every victory in the game symbolizes a step towards recovery in real life.
